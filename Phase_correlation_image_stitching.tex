\subsection {Fourier Transform}
The definition of the Fourier Transform of a function \( f(x) \) is given by:
% insert the formula for Fourier Transform
\begin{equation}
F(k) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x) e^{-2\pi i k x} dx
\end{equation}
where \( F(k) \) is the Fourier Transform of \( f(x) \), and \( k \) is the frequency variable. The inverse Fourier Transform is defined as:
\begin{equation}
f(x) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} F(k) e^{2\pi i k x} dk
\end{equation}
